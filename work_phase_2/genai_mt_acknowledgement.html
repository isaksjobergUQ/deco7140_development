GenAI & MT Acknowledgement

What I used GenAI/MT for in WP2, how I checked it, and what I learned.

Where I used GenAI/MT

  • Persona prompts and interview scripting (drafting and phrasing).
  • Summarising WCAG and ACS ethics into short checklists.
  • Refactoring nav to a simple JS module and CSS tweaks.
  • Colors and fonts for the website.

How I verified & adapted

  • Checked WCAG items against course notes; kept only criteria relevant to our users.
  • Rewrote in plain English; removed any jargon.
  • Tested mobile nav and focus states manually.

Risks & limits

  • Possible inaccuracies or bias in summaries → I cross-checked with lecture slides.
  • Code suggestions weren’t tailored to my CSS → I simplified and tested on mobile.

What I learned

  • Short, specific criteria beat long lists when designing for real users.
  • Simple nav logic in JS is clearer than complex CSS tricks.
  • Plain language + visible focus helps everyone, not just assistive tech users.
