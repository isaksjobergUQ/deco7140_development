Cultural Communication Tips

Understanding cultural differences in communication can help you connect better with others:

🇦🇺 Australian Communication Style

  • Australians tend to be direct but friendly
  • Use of informal language and slang is common
  • Eye contact shows respect and attention
  • Punctuality is highly valued

🎓 Academic Communication

  • Use formal language in emails to professors
  • Be specific and clear in your requests
  • Ask questions if you don't understand
  • Participate actively in class discussions

🤝 Building Relationships

  • Show genuine interest in others' cultures
  • Be patient with language barriers
  • Share your own cultural background
  • Join cultural clubs and events
