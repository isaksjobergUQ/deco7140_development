Work Phase 3 - Design Proposal

A comprehensive style guide and design proposal for an international student platform

About This Site

This website presents my design proposal for A3 Assessment, focusing on creating an engaging and accessible web experience for international students. The design emphasizes inclusivity, language support, and user-centered principles to help international students navigate their new environment.

International Student Focus Area

This design proposal centers around creating a digital platform where international students can share tips, discover cultural events, access resources, and provide peer-to-peer advice. The website aims to foster inclusivity, support language diversity, and create a supportive community for students navigating life in a new country.

Design Goals

The primary objectives for this international student platform include:

  • Creating an intuitive and accessible user experience for diverse language backgrounds
  • Implementing responsive design for mobile-first international students
  • Establishing a welcoming visual identity that reflects cultural inclusivity
  • Supporting multiple languages and cultural contexts
  • Facilitating peer-to-peer knowledge sharing and community building
  • Ensuring fast loading times for students with varying internet access
  • Maintaining accessibility standards for users with different abilities

Site Structure

This design proposal includes comprehensive documentation across multiple pages:

  • Site Map: Overall website structure and navigation
  • Style Guide: Typography, colors, and design patterns
  • Visceral Design: Emotional and aesthetic considerations
  • Behavioural Design: Usability and interaction patterns
  • Reflective Design: Long-term user engagement strategies
  • Ethical Response: Responsible design considerations
