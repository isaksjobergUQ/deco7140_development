Style Guide

Design system and visual guidelines for the international student platform

Design Levels

Our design approach follows Norman's three levels of design, each addressing different aspects of user experience and interaction with the platform.

Visceral Design

Designing for first impressions covering layout, typography, and color schemes that create immediate emotional responses.

Behavioural Design

Creating intuitive interactions and usability patterns that support user tasks and enhance the overall experience.

Reflective Design

Building long-term engagement through community features, personalization, and cultural exchange facilitation.
